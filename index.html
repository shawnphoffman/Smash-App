Smash App

Companion app for the boardgame, Smash Up, and its related expansions.

Smash App is an UNOFFICIAL companion app for the popular Smash Up card game. With Smash App you are able to keep track of all the players and their scores throughout your games. You can even keep track of what factions you want to play with and randomly generate pairings for an adding dimension to your play. Do you ever forget if you did or did not add up the points from the most recent base card? Well now you have the ability to review points for individual users as well as the entire game session to make sure everything is on track. Smash App is a must-have for your next game night.

View the Project on GitHub shawnphoffman/Smash-App

Features:

  • Add/Edit/Delete Players
  • Enable Factions you want to play with / currently own
  • Randomize enabled factions and pair them with current players
  • Add/Subtract/Review points for the current game session and individual players
